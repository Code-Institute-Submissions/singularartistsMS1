IRELAND'S LEADING INDEPENDENT MUSIC PROMOTER

Singular Artists is an exciting and credible independent event promotions company encompassing booking and management services. The added strength of international partners Kilimanjaro Live and DEAG elevates a whole new level of excitement to the venture.

30+ YEARS OF INDUSTRY EXPERTISE

The company comprises of three experienced promoters, Fin O’Leary, Brian Hand and Simon Merriman, who whilst at Aiken Promotions had helped the company grow its reputation and roster in the Irish live music business. With over 30 years of combined experience and expertise, the dynamic team have firmly cemented their place in the industry.

ARTIST-FIRST ETHOS

Singular Artists has built the foundations of the company on an ethos that the trio all believe in – a mission of putting the artist first. They proudly represent a number of artists internationally as well as using their long-established relationships to source and provide talent for venues, festivals and events worldwide.
